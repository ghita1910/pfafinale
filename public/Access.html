Why Web Accessibility Matters

Inclusive User Experience

Web accessibility ensures that everyone, including those with disabilities, can access and interact with your website.

Legal Compliance

Many countries have laws that require websites to be accessible. Failing to comply could result in legal consequences.

Wider Audience Reach

By making your website accessible, you open it up to a broader audience, including people with visual, auditory, or motor impairments.
